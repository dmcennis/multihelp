Aggregator Appendix

All available aggregators are listed in the aggregators table (accessible by the alter aggregators button). There are two classes of aggregators - general and specific.

General Aggregators

General aggregators apply to every feature chosen. As such, they do not need a list of specific features to aggregate. These aggregators may still have parameters to set, thus editing a aggregator is possible whether or not specific fetaures are set.

Specific Aggregators

Specific aggregators apply only to a subset of features. These aggregators include a feature selection dialog in the edit aggregators box.
